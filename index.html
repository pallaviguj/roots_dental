About Us

At Roots Dental Speciality Clinic, your oral health is our top priority. It is led by Dr. Aarti Bohora (BDS, MDS, Ph.D), who brings 15+ years of experience, we provide comprehensive dental care using the latest technology and modern techniques. Our facility is equipped to handle all aspects of dentistry including Cosmetic Dentistry, Root Canal Treatments, Emergency Dentistry, Implantology, Periodontology, Prosthodontics, Orthodontics, and Facial Aesthetic Treatments. Whether you're here for a routine check-up or a complex procedure, we're committed to delivering a sound and soothing experience that prioritizes both your comfort and long-term oral health.

Roots dental speciality clinic aims to ensure the natural and beautiful look be the top most priority without anyone noticing the difference.
