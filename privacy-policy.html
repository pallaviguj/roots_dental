Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 25, 2026

Roots Dental Speciality is committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

By using this website, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Users are encouraged to review this page periodically.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following personal information:

a) Information you provide voluntarily

  • Name

  • Appointment details
  • Any information submitted through contact or appointment forms

b) Automatically collected information

  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Pages visited

If medical or health-related information is collected through forms, it will be treated as Sensitive Personal Data and handled with strict confidentiality.

Our website may use cookies to improve user experience and analyze website traffic.

Cookies help us understand:

  • Which pages are visited

You may choose to disable cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some features of the website.

Third-party services such as Google Analytics may also use cookies, over which we have no direct control.

We use the information collected for the following purposes:

  • To improve website functionality
  • For legal and regulatory compliance

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties.

4. Data Protection and Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

However, transmission of information over the internet is not completely secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security of data transmitted online.

Our website may contain links to external websites. Roots Dental Speciality is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of such websites. Users should review the privacy policies of third-party websites before providing personal data.

You have the right to:

  • Withdraw consent
  • Request deletion of data (subject to legal obligations)

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details below.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law.
